For this project I was tasked with producing photography for a Photobook. Here, I created a series of abstract images focusing on the quality of light on my subject, a Christmas tree. As you can probably guess, this was during christmas time and so I tried my best to try to capture several different angles that would help me produce some good abstract photos. Though photoshop was still used here, this particular project helped more so in developing my photography skills than anything else I would say.

The project dealt with abstract photography. This more or less consisted of taking shots of objects or places from angles or perspectives, maybe not so common, or at least to try to do so in such a way that it makes the viewer disassociate the image from the material world so to speak. Perhaps either by displaying movement and focusing on that or displaying none and taking a kind of close up shot that may paint the subject in a different light when photographed. This project was one of the ways in which I kept learning more about photography as well as color correction in photoshop, as I went on throughout the course this work comes from.

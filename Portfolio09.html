In this project I had to choose a topic or area of interest, that can include any area of typographic execution that consistently uses a particular typeface, family, or typographic characteristic and create and design a book (and subsequently a book layout) that displays several examples of the typefaces that this topic employs as well as include a few type analyses within it. So for me, the topic that I chose was comic books and so I basically centered my typographic book around that. I did some sketching as well as plenty of research and image searching in order to get enough of the information that I needed and enough pages as was required as well. Overall, this dealt mainly with layout and so there were a few magazine type books and the like that were looked at for some inspiration, but I would say that my skills in that area certainly improved through this project.
